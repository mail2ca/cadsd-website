Our Journey

Two Decades of Trusted Practice

Founded in 2003 in New Delhi, DSD & Co. was established with a clear purpose — to provide businesses with not just compliance support, but genuine financial partnership. Over the past 22 years, our firm has grown into a comprehensive Chartered Accountancy practice, built on a foundation of integrity, technical excellence and a deep commitment to our clients' financial well-being.

What began as a focused tax and audit practice has evolved to encompass business advisory, litigation support, forensic accounting and — most recently — AI-powered financial process automation. We have consistently embraced change, adopted technology and expanded our expertise to stay ahead of an evolving regulatory and business landscape.

Today, DSD & Co. serves clients across Manufacturing, Retail, Services, Startups and Banking sectors — bringing the same dedication, attention and quality of service that has defined our practice since inception.

Meet Our Partners

Each of our partners brings decades of specialised expertise and a deep commitment to client service across all areas of Chartered Accountancy practice.

DS
Dheeraj Sharma
Fellow Chartered Accountant
FCA FAFP (ICAI) Certified New Age Auditor 20+ Years Experience

Dheeraj Sharma is a Fellow Chartered Accountant with over 20 years of experience, specialising in direct taxation, forensic audit and fraud prevention. He holds the prestigious Forensic Audit and Fraud Prevention (FAFP) certification from ICAI and is a Certified New Age Auditor — credentials that place him at the forefront of modern audit practice.

With a strong grasp of technical chart analysis and algorithmic development, Dheeraj brings a unique blend of financial expertise and analytical thinking to complex tax assessments, litigation matters and forensic engagements. He leads the firm's direct tax, audit and technology-driven practice areas.

SG
Surender Garg
Fellow Chartered Accountant
FCA DISA (ICAI) Tax Litigation Expert 22+ Years Experience

Surender Garg is a Fellow Chartered Accountant with over 22 years of practice, widely recognised for his expertise in tax litigation and dispute resolution. He holds the Diploma in Information Systems Audit (DISA) from ICAI, enabling him to handle system audits and information security-related engagements with equal proficiency.

Surender leads the firm's litigation practice, representing clients before income tax authorities, appellate tribunals and GST adjudication forums. His combination of technical tax knowledge and procedural expertise makes him a trusted advisor for businesses navigating complex tax disputes and regulatory challenges.

DS
Deepak Singhal
Fellow Chartered Accountant & Advocate
FCA DISA (ICAI) LLB 23+ Years Experience

Deepak Singhal is a Fellow Chartered Accountant and a qualified Advocate with over 23 years of experience — the most senior partner at DSD & Co. His dual qualification as an FCA and LLB, combined with a DISA certification, gives him a uniquely comprehensive perspective on financial, legal and information systems matters.

Deepak oversees the firm's compliance, audit and advisory practices, bringing a legally grounded approach to GST advisory, corporate law compliance, ROC filings and complex multi-jurisdictional engagements. His ability to view financial matters through both a CA and legal lens is a significant advantage for clients facing regulatory or legal challenges.
