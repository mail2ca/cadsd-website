Partner-Led Service

At DSD & Co., every client engagement is led directly by a qualified CA partner. You always work with the most experienced professional in the room — not a junior associate. This ensures accountability, depth and personalised attention at every stage.

Technology-Driven Practice

We are among the few CA firms in Delhi that actively use Python, Power Query and AI tools in day-to-day practice. This enables us to automate reconciliations, generate reports faster and deliver more accurate outcomes — at no extra cost to clients.

Our partners collectively hold FCA, DISA, FAFP, LLB and Certified New Age Auditor credentials. This breadth of qualification enables us to handle complex multi-domain engagements — from forensic investigations to legal advisory — all in-house.
